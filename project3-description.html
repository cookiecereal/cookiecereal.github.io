IT & Computer Building - Personal Hobby

Hobby Overview

Based in El Paso, TX, I have developed a passion for IT and computer building. This hobby allows me to apply my technical skills, stay updated with the latest hardware trends, and help others with their computing needs.

Key Activities

  • PC Modernization: Converted an old PC into a modern gaming computer by replacing and upgrading its hardware from scratch.
  • Hardware Installation: Assist relatives and friends with hardware installations, from selecting parts to complete system assembly.
  • Technical Support: Provide help with software installation and troubleshooting for various clients.

Skills Developed

  • Hardware Knowledge: Extensive understanding of computer components and their compatibility.
  • Problem-Solving: Ability to diagnose and fix both hardware and software issues.
  • Customer Service: Experience in understanding client needs and providing tailored solutions.
  • Technology Trends: Keeping up-to-date with the latest advancements in computer hardware and software.

Notable Projects

Gaming PC Conversion

Successfully transformed an outdated PC into a high-performance gaming rig. This project involved:

  • Assessing the existing hardware and identifying upgrade opportunities.
  • Researching and selecting compatible, performance-enhancing components.
  • Carefully disassembling the old system and installing new parts.
  • Optimizing the system for gaming performance.

Community Tech Support

Regularly assist friends, family, and local community members with their IT needs, including:

  • Recommending and installing hardware upgrades.
  • Setting up new computers and peripherals.
  • Troubleshooting software issues and removing malware.
  • Providing guidance on proper computer maintenance and security practices.

Future Goals

As I continue to pursue this hobby, I aim to:

  • Expand my knowledge into more specialized areas like custom water cooling and overclocking.
  • Explore small-form-factor (SFF) PC building techniques.
  • Contribute to online tech communities and forums to share knowledge and learn from others.
